Scoops Ahoy - Real Time Food Delivery System

Scoops Ahoy is a dynamic and user-friendly application designed to revolutionize the ice cream ordering experience. As a modern ice cream shop platform, it enables users to easily sign up or log in, browse a comprehensive menu, add items to their cart, and place orders seamlessly. One of the standout features of Scoops Ahoy is its real-time delivery tracking system, powered by Google Maps API, which allows users to track their orders on a map and receive timely notifications upon delivery. This application not only enhances the customer experience but also ensures a smooth and efficient ordering process.

Developed with a strong focus on adhering to industry-standard design principles, Scoops Ahoy showcases exemplary software engineering practices. The application employs the SOLID principles to ensure robust, maintainable, and scalable code. Key design patterns, such as the Observer pattern and Dependency Injection, are used to promote flexibility and decoupling within the system. By leveraging Ably Pub/Sub Channels for real-time updates and integrating PostgreSQL for reliable data management, Scoops Ahoy is a testament to modern web development and effective application design.
